FIX IT

Assembling and Disassembling It is a platform for the students to check their creative and innovative thinking and take it to the next level. It consists of three rounds in which the difficulty increases at every level and only people with an innovative thinking can make it to the finals.

FIRST ROUND - The first round consists of a simple puzzle after which a a certain number of participants will be eliminated.

SECOND ROUND - It consists of mechanical parts which are to be disassembled & assembled.

THIRD ROUND - It is the final round in which various parts such as gear mechanisms, mechatronics, engine components, electrical components are to be dismantled in a given time period.
